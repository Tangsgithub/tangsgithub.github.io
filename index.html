Logistics

Breakbulk (BBK) & OOG Shipping

For large-diameter SSAW/LSAW pipes up to 160" and 18 meters in length, standard containers are inadequate. Our dedicated logistics team has rich experience in charting Breakbulk Vessels and handling Out of Gauge (OOG) cargo, ensuring safe loading, dunnage, and securing to prevent any deformation during sea rolling.

Documentation

Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Absolute transparency is our promise. Read the most critical answers before initiating your next multi-million dollar procurement.

Are you a real manufacturer or just a trading company?

We are a leading manufacturer with our own extensive production facilities for ERW, LSAW, and SSAW pipes in China. We warmly welcome video calls for virtual factory tours or physical site visits by your EPC team.

What is your claim policy if products arrive damaged?

We take full responsibility for our quality. If damage occurs due to our packing or manufacturing, we require photos and a TPI report at the destination port. We will compensate or replace the defective goods without evasion.

Have you exported to our region before?

We have a strong track record supplying top EPC contractors globally, including the Middle East (Saudi Arabia, UAE), South America, and Southeast Asia. We can share specific reference lists upon request.

Can you provide MTC EN10204 3.1 or 3.2?

Yes, we provide full traceability. Every shipment comes with an MTC strictly complying with EN10204 3.1. We can also provide EN10204 3.2 certified by third parties like TUV or ABS upon request.

Do you have Breakbulk Vessel shipping experience?

Absolutely. For large-diameter pipes that exceed standard container dimensions, our dedicated logistics team is highly experienced in Breakbulk (BBK) and Out of Gauge (OOG) shipments.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ typically starts from 10 to 25 metric tons depending on the specific pipe dimensions. However, for project trials or stock items, we can accommodate smaller quantities.

What are your standard payment terms?

We generally accept T/T (Telegraphic Transfer) with a 30% deposit and 70% balance against the copy of B/L, or 100% Irrevocable L/C at sight from reputable banks.

How long is your typical delivery time?

For standard sizes in stock, delivery takes 7-15 days. For newly produced items or custom specifications, lead times range from 30 to 45 days after deposit confirmation.

How do you package pipes for sea freight?

Pipes are bundled with strong steel strips. Ends are protected with plastic caps or bevel protectors. We also offer waterproof wrapping, bare packing, or custom wooden crates.

What certifications do your products hold?

Our facilities are ISO certified. Our products strictly comply with international standards like API 5L, API 5CT, ASTM, EN, DIN, and hold CE and PED certificates.

Do you accept third-party inspections (TPI)?

Yes, absolutely. To guarantee 100% quality, we confidently accept and fully cooperate with renowned agencies such as TUV, SGS, Intertek, ABS, and LR prior to any shipment.

What anti-corrosion processing do you provide?

We provide advanced anti-corrosion solutions including 3PE/3LPE coating, FBE coating, Epoxy coating, Hot Dipped Galvanized (HDG), and specialized thermal insulation coatings.

Can you provide custom lengths and ends?

Absolutely. We offer precision cutting to exact lengths (e.g., 5.8m, 6m, 11.8m). We also provide beveled ends, threaded ends with couplings, and grooved ends.

Do you provide free samples?

Yes, we can provide small, standard-sized samples for free for quality check. However, the international courier freight cost is typically borne by the buyer.

How often do you update your steel prices?

Chinese steel prices fluctuate based on raw material costs and exchange rates. Our official quotations are generally valid for 3 to 7 days.

ERW vs Seamless (SMLS) pipes?

Seamless pipes can withstand higher pressures, ideal for critical oil & gas applications. ERW pipes are welded, more cost-effective, and suitable for low-to-medium pressure fluid delivery.

Strict Quality Control & Processing

Complying with ISO 9001, we provide 100% inspection and advanced anti-corrosion solutions for global EPC projects.

100% Inspection Process

  • Raw Material Check: Comprehensive verification of chemical composition and mechanical properties before production.
  • In-Process Control: Continuous monitoring and NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) including Ultrasonic Testing (UT) to ensure zero defects.
  • Pre-Shipment Inspection: Final checks on quantity, markings, packaging, and unblemished appearance prior to dispatch.
  • Third-Party Approval: We confidently accept and cooperate with TPIs such as TUV, SGS, Intertek, ABS, and LR.
